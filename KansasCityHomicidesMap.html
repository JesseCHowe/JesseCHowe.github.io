Homicide Trends: 1960-2016

Like most metropolitan areas in the United States, Kansas City has seen waves of high homicide rates followed by relative lows. The War on drugs begining in the 1980s and spilling over into the early 90s saw some of the highest rates of homicide in the city's history. Over the past twenty years homicides are on the decline. Unfortunately Kansas City is seeing a slower rate than most cities. At the moment 2017 is on track to pass the number of homicides from the previous year.
