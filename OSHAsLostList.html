OSHA's lost list

When companies fail to recognize or comply with health or safety regulations, it can lead to fines by the Occupational Safety and Health Administration. These fines are often trivial for the companies, with serious violations capped at $12,675 and willful violations at $126,749. The real leverage OSHA has are their press releases. With a meager workforce of 2,000 inspectors nationwide a strong press release can send a message to other companies in the same geographic area and industry. Since January 18th OSHA hasn't released a single press releases on it's website, but thanks to Jordan Barab, a former OSHA employee, a list is being maintained on his personal blog.
